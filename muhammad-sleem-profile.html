About

Where clinical practice meets applied AI

I spent years reasoning through rehabilitation cases before I started reasoning through data pipelines — and the two disciplines turned out to share the same instincts: observe carefully, form a hypothesis, test it, and stay honest about what the evidence actually shows. That's the lens I bring to every model, dashboard, and application I build.

My work spans machine learning and deep learning, computer vision and explainable AI, data analytics and business intelligence, and the frontend interfaces that make all of it usable by someone who isn't reading the code.

Build systems that are technically rigorous, understandable to the people using them, and useful in real-world settings.
